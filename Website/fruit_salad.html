Fruit Salad
35 minutes
4 Servings
Ingredients
  • 1
    banana
  • 1
    cup grapes
  • 1
    cup strawberries
  • ¼
    cup orange juice
Directions
  • Slice 1 banana and add to a large bowl.
  • Add 1 cup of grapes to bowl.
  • Slice 1 cup of strawberries and add to bowl.
  • Pour ¼ cups of orange juice over the fruit and mix to coat.
  • Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
Chef's Note
Such a refreshing treat on a hot summer day! I make this recipe a lot and it never fails. The combination of these 3 simple fruits are just heavenly.
